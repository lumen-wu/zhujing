## Themes emerging

- **Start from yourself** — all three essays: you are the ground, not the external
- **Phenomena as ground** — we access phenomena, not Truth; meaning isn't out there
- **Constraint enables connection** — being stuck in individual perspective makes empathy possible
- **Internal vs external** — meaning is generated, not found; seeking externally causes absurdity
- **Renormalization** — macro patterns are real, not just convenient fictions
